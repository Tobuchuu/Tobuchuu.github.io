Top 5 places to visit in China

China has lot's of nice places to visit. Here are our picks for the top 5 places you must visit on your trip!


  1. The Great Wall of China in Shanghai

    An easy pick for this list is The Great Wall of China. It is located near Shanghai, and it's over 6000 km long. It is probably one of the most well known locations in China.

    Most tours choose to go to the section near Badaling Pass, as it is easy to get to.




  2. The Forbidden City / The Imperial Palace in Beijing

    This massive palace in Beijing is very important. It was built between 1406 and 1420, and it has been the residence of 24 Emperors.

    This place is so big, you will most likely have to set aside a whole day to be able to see everything it has to show.




  3. The Terracotta Army in Xi'an

    In the 1970s, some farmers accidentally found something huge while digging. They had found the first Emperors tomb, and over 8000 statues made of terracotta, guarding the tomb.

    Many say that this is one of the coolest experiences you can have in China.




  4. Leshan Giant Buddha in Sichuan

    In the 1970s, some farmers accidentally found something huge while digging. They had found the first Emperors tomb, and over 8000 statues made of terracotta, guarding the tomb.

    Many say that this is one of the coolest experiences you can have in China.
